What Changed

From manual re-keying to instant, hands-off routing

BeforeManual, error-prone, slow
  • ✕Staff copied leads by hand between systems
  • ✕Field mapping broke — purchase dates, models and serials landed in the wrong place
  • ✕Leads sat unassigned; the median lead waited ~2.5 days for first contact
  • ✕Duplicates slipped through and cluttered the list
  • ✕Speed-to-lead was never measured at all
AfterAutomated, accurate, instant
  • ✓Submissions flow in automatically — zero re-keying
  • ✓Purchase date, model and serial captured to the correct fields every time
  • ✓Each lead routed to the right local agent the moment it arrives
  • ✓Strict duplicate checker blocks repeats before they enter
  • ✓Speed-to-lead auto-calculated on every record speed_to_lead_minutes
↑

The opportunity this unlocks: in the current data only 8% of leads were contacted within an hour and 40% within a day. Instant routing removes the assignment delay that caused most of that lag.

Next Steps

Moving from the duplicate sheet to live production

1

Validate in sandbox

Sign off routing, de-duplication, timestamps and warranty mapping against a set of test cases.

2

Freeze & back up live

Take a dated snapshot of the production master before any change — your instant rollback point.

3

Port scripts & triggers

Copy the Apps Script project and installable triggers across to the live sheet.

4

Repoint integrations

Aim Bit Integrations and the CF7 forms (incl. the Postcode field) at the live tabs.

5

Controlled go-live

Parallel-run for 1–2 weeks and watch the Logs tab to confirm clean, error-free processing.

6

Retire the manual process

Train agents on the update links and set a refresh cadence for the dashboard.
